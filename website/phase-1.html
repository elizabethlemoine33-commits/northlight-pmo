What AEO Is
AI Engine Optimization (AEO / GEO) engineers content so that large language models — ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews — correctly ingest, cite, and recommend Northlight Advisory Services. The goal is not search ranking but brand representation in zero-click AI answers.

Key Decisions
Platform: Custom flat HTML
Full control over schema, meta, llms.txt, and file structure without CMS overhead
FAQ page: sitemap + llms.txt only, no nav link
AI-facing entity disambiguation page — humans who want it can find it via search
llms.txt automation: GitHub Action (not manual script)
Blog list stays current automatically on push; no manual step to remember
Full entity name in extractable copy
"Northlight Advisory Services" (not just "Northlight") in all AI-extractable sentences to prevent conflation with theatre company and software products
Geographic anchor: Halifax, Nova Scotia, Canada
Distinguishes NAS from other "Northlight" or "Northlight Advisory Services" entities in AI model training data
