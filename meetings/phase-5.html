Decisions Log
# Decision Why Date
1 ZDR language over ad hoc retention claim The original claim was technically accurate but vague. ZDR is an account-level setting that is verifiable in Groq's Data Controls panel — naming it precisely is more defensible and more honest. 2026-07-06
2 Lightest-touch consent (toggle + nudge banner, no enforcement) Recording consent laws vary by jurisdiction and most require only one-party consent. A Settings checkbox with a pre-recording nudge meets the legal reminder goal without blocking the workflow or creating friction for users in one-party jurisdictions. 2026-07-06
3 Proposal Mode moved to section 01 in case study The original structure positioned Meetings as "a better Otter.ai." The actual job-to-be-done is replacing proposal-writing labour — a category of one. Leading with that differentiator changes the product's perceived value tier entirely. 2026-07-06
4 localStorage for consent toggle state (no IPC) The toggle is a UI preference with no security implications. localStorage persists across Electron sessions without requiring main-process IPC or safeStorage changes — keeping the change minimal and self-contained. 2026-07-06
